ስለ እኛ/about us

Ethiel Maternal and Child Health Speciality Center is a healthcare facility or hospital dedicated providing specialized medical care and services to expectant mothers, infants, and children. This center play a crucial role in promoting and maintaining the health and well-being of both mothers and their children.

የምንሰጣቸዉ አገልግሎቶች/Our Services

Complete Hematology Including ESR/ESR ን ጨምሮ የተሟላ የደም ህክምናh6>

Complete Blood Count (CBC): Hemoglobin (Hb) concentration, Hematocrit (Hct) level, Red Blood Cell (RBC) count, White Blood Cell (WBC) count, Platelet count Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR): Measures the rate at which red blood cells settle in a tube over a specific time period (typically 1 hour).

Complete Serology Including Quantitive CRP/CRPን ጨምሮ የተሟላ ሴሮሎጂ

Quantitative C-Reactive Protein (CRP): Measures the concentration of CRP in the blood, Elevated CRP levels can indicate inflammation, infection, or other underlying health issues Antinuclear Antibody (ANA) Test: Detects antibodies.

Complete Stool and Urine Analysis/የተሟላ የሰገራ እና የሽንት ትንተና

Both stool and urine analyses are valuable diagnostic tools used by healthcare professionals to assess a patient's overall health, detect gastrointestinal disorders, kidney problems, infections, and other medical conditions.

Complete Blood Chemistry Including Lipid profile/የ Lipid መገለጫን ጨምሮ የተሟላ የደም ኬሚስትሪ

used to assess overall health, screen for various medical conditions (such as diabetes, liver disease, and kidney problems).

Hormone Analysis/የሆርሞን ትንተና

Thyroid Hormones: Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH), Free Thyroxine (T4), Triiodothyronine (T3) Sex Hormones: Follicle-Stimulating Hormone (FSH), Luteinizing Hormone (LH), Progesterone (in females)

Prenatal Care/ቅድመ ወሊድ እንክብካቤ

Maternal health assessments, Monitoring of fetal development, Ultrasound examinations, Nutritional counseling for expectant mothers, Education on pregnancy and childbirth

Pediatric Care/የሕፃናት ሕክምና

Well-baby check-ups, Childhood vaccinations and immunizations, Treatment of common childhood illnesses and injuries, Developmental assessments, Growth monitoring

Family Planning and Reproductive Health/የቤተሰብ እቅድ እና የስነ ተዋልዶ ጤና

Contraceptive counseling and services, Sexual health education, Pap smears and gynecological care for women

Education and Support/ትምህርት እና ድጋፍ

Parenting classes and support groups, Breastfeeding support, Nutrition education, Child safety and injury prevention education
